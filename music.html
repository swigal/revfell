About Steadfast Music Ministries

Every since I began working in worship and music ministries at church, the word steadfast has been one word I kept coming back to. According to dictionary.com, steadfast means to be fixed or firm in a direction or purpose – unwavering. The the word for steadfast in the original Hebrew is chesed (pronounced kheh’-sed) and it refers to God’s devout loving-kindness, mercy, and faithfulness at the very core of His character and actions.

God is steadfast. Therefore, His love is steadfast – unwavering, faithful, and kind.

In recent months this word has come back to the front of my mind. As we have stepped out in faith, defending what we we believe, defending the Scriptures, we are embodying the very meaning of steadfast. We are standing firm, resolute, unwavering on the scriptures. We are placing our faith in God. We are remaining steadfast in God's grace and love, revealing His Word, Jesus Christ, to the world we serve in, with the goal to bring everyone into fellowship. God will always remain steadfast in his love for us. So, we must remain steadfast in our love for him. ~ Samuel Wigal

Your lovingkindness is better than life, my lips will praise You. ~ Psalm 63:4 (TLV)

Our Mission:

Revealing the Word of God...
Bringing everyone into fellowship...

Here at Revelation Fellowship, God’s Word is the foundation our beliefs. All our teaching, preaching, study, and learning begins in God’s Word. We don’t conform the scripture to our beliefs, we conform our beliefs to the scripture. Jesus Christ is the Word of God, and He is the Way, the Truth, and the Life. Jesus is The Word, Jesus is God, and God does not change. Neither shall the scriptures to fit an ever-changing world. We stand firm on God’s Word as our inerrant foundation.
