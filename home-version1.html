Welcome to Revelation Fellowship GMC

Your journey begins here. We are a community dedicated to spiritual growth and fellowship. God's Word is our inspiration for living out our faith and encourages us to REVEAL the Word, Jesus Christ, to all the world and welcome everyone into FELLOWSHIP with Him.

Sunday School

—  9:30 AM  —

We have Sunday School classes for all ages. From Pre-K to Adult, we all have something to learn about our great God.

"At many times and in many ways, God spoke long ago to the fathers through the prophets. In these last days He has spoken to us through a Son, whom He appointed heir of all things and through whom He created the universe."

Hebrews 1:1-2 (TLV)

Sunday Worship

—  10:45 AM & 6:30 PM  —
