Revealing the Word of God...

The Word of God is revealed through preaching, teaching, study, prayer, worship, and many other daily tasks. We strive to seek God in the scriptures and our everyday lives. In the scripture we find truth. When we accept the truth of God's Word we find love. When we accept the love of God, we find fellowship.

Bringing Everyone into Fellowship...

In fellowship with God and each other we are made stronger. We are able to stand up against a world that seeks to break us down. We stand strong on the truth of God's Word in the love of Jesus Christ surrounded by our community of faith.

Sunday School — 9:30 AM

We have Sunday school classes for everyone from children, to youth, to adults. We encourage everyone to join a class. Sunday School is an important time to not only fellowship with friends and family, but to learn about the great God we serve.

Sunday Morning Worship — 10:45 AM

Our main time of worship is at 10:45 AM on Sunday mornings. Singing hymns and songs of praise, sharing in acts of worship, praising God and praying for our community, hearing the Word of God preached, we welcome you to join us.

Graceful Gatherings COMING FALL 2025

Starting Fall of 2025 we will be launching new Sunday evening worsip services. Worship will begin at 5:00 PM with every together for a time of family-style worship and a devotion led by Pastor Stacy. Then we will break off into small groups. Children and youth will go to their small groups and adults will go to their small groups. In these small groups we will focus on discussion about the topic for the day, work on crafts for missions, and fellowship with oneanother to build each other up in faith and support each other through times of struggle. We hope you wil join us this Fall!
